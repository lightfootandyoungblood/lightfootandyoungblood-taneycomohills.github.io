Taneycomo Hills

Six log cabins nestled amidst the woods above Downing St. in Hollister, Missouri.

Staying in the cabins on Taneycomo Hills Dr. gives you all the quietserenity of staying deep in the forest but you are only 5 minutes from the Branson Landing.

These cabins, formerly known as Lonesome Dove, have been very successful operating as a short term rental investment.

The 6 cabins consist of one 3 bedroom cabin, two 2 bedroom cabins and 3 one bedroom cabins.

Vacant land entices great value add with this property.

There is room to add additional cabins, swimming pool, glamping or whatever your heart desires.
